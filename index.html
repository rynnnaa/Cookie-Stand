WHAT ARE SALMON COOKIES?

Salmon cookies were discovered when Pat the owner was at home one night making salmon for dinner and cookies for her daughter's bake sale. That's when she had an idea! "What if I made cookies out of Salmon?" Kind of a weird idea, but that one idea has now become a delicious sensation here in the Pacific Northwest! Come one, come all, to the various locations now offered around surroundings areas!
